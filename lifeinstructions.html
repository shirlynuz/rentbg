Instructions

The Game of Life

Setting up

  1. Shuffle the Life tiles and take four and place them near Millionaire Estates
  2. Separate the other cards into 4 piles: Salary, House Deeds, Career and Stocks (do the same for Homeowner's insurance policies, bank loans and automobile Insurance policies
  3. Choose a banker, give each player $10000
  4. Each player to choose a car. Spin the wheel, player with highest number goes first
  5. Choose you want to begin a career or go to college

Find out about the difference between a career and college

Spinning the wheel

• Spin the wheel and move ahead the number of spaces

• You may choose to buy stocks or insurance and take out loans from the bank

Tiles

What is the meaning of the different tile colours?

• Green= pay days, receive salary when you land on them

• Blue=Follow the instructions on the space if you want to

• Orange=No choice but to follow the instructions

• Red=Stop on the space even if you still have moves left

• Landing on a Life space = take one Life tile from the pile

• Landing on a Career space = pay the person who owns the career/pay to bank if no one owns it

• Landing on Buy a House space = Stop and purchase a home (draw from the House Deeds pile)

How to win?

• Choose to retire either at Millionaire Estates or Countryside Acres

• If you retire at Millionaire Estates, you have the chance to receive 4 Life tiles if you are the richest person to retire there

At the end of the game, players to return loans and add up their Life tiles and money. Richest player wins
