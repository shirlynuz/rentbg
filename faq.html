❔ FAQs ❔

What is the price of rental?
Our standard pricing is $4 per day per game. If you rent 2 games for 1 day, the price will be $8. Extension of games is also $4 per day. Simply call our hotline at 6775 1133
When must I return the game?
Within 24h after the end of the rental. For example, if you rent the game on 1 Jan for 1 day, you have to return by 2 Jan
What is the timeframe of collection?
The timeframe is within 1 day. If you rent on 2 Jan, you can collect it on 1 Jan. This also means that there is no rent imposed on collection day and return day
Is there a deposit required?
Yes. There will be a $1 deposit imposed on your credit card
What happens if I do not return the game on time?
There will be a charge of $3 per day for every day that you are late in returning the game
Where do I collect or return?
Collect at Singapore Polytechnic or opt for delivery. However, all games must be returned at Singapore Polytechnic.
