Instructions

Risk The Game of Global Domination

Setting up

  • Select a colour and count out the "armies"
  • If 3 are playing, each player counts out 35 Infantry.
  • If 4 are playing, each player counts out 30 Infantry.
  • If 5 are playing, each player counts out 25 Infantry.
  • If 6 are playing, each player counts out 20 Infantry.
  • Roll one die. Whoever rolls the highest number takes one Infantry piece from his or her pile and places it onto any territory on the board, thus claiming that territory. Everyone takes turn to place one army onto any unoccupied territory until all 42 territories are claimed.
  • Each player then places one additional army onto their occupied territory until they run out
  • Each turn consists of 3 steps (getting and placing new armies, attacking and fortifying your position)

How many new armies received per turn?

Number of territories occupied/3=number of armies received, place new armies on any occupied terriroties

Number of armies received for each controlled continent (must occupy all the territory in that continent):

  • Asia: 7
  • North America: 5
  • Europe: 5
  • Africa: 3
  • South America: 2
  • Australia: 2

Cards

• You earn 1 Risk card when you capture at least one territory

• Collect 3 cards in any of the combinations: 3 cards of same design (infantry, artillery, cavalry), 1 each of the 3 design, any 2 + "wild" card

• After collecting these sets, you can trade in the cards.

Find out more about the trading, attacking fortifying your position

You win when you occupy all territories on the board and eliminate all your opponents
